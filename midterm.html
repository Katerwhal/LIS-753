Friends of Palo Alto Library Book Sale

Where can you find us?

The booksale takes place inside Cubberly Community Center. We will be in three rooms:

  • Main room: along the north driveway, adjacent to the Charleston shopping center.
  • Children's items: near the cubberly track, pavilion, and the greendell school.
  • Bargain books: rooms K6 and K7.

When will we be there?

Saturday

  • Childrens room and bargain room: opens at 10am.
  • Main room: ticket distribution starts at 8am, room opens at 11am.

Sunday

  • All rooms open at 11am.

What tickets?

  • Two numbered tickets will be distributed per person for entry into the main room on Saturday, starting at 8am.
  • You may leave and return before room opens and numbers are called, but missing your number forfeits your place in line.
  • In this case, or if you don't have a ticket, you may join to latter part of the line.

What will it cost?

  • Book prices are 50 cents or 1 dollar in the main room and children's room.
  • Bargain books are 25 cents, 50 cents, or 1 dollar, with everything in the bargain room being half off on Sunday.

Why buy from us?

  • 100% of proceeds go to benefit the library!
  • No sales tax!
  • Browse over 50,000 items, from music and movies to school-age fiction and non-english books, and much more!

What will happen?

Saturday

  • During the first hour of sale (11-12), customers may choose no more than 12 books in main and childrens room.
  • Children's and bargain rooms are open one hour earlier (10am) than main room (11am).

Sunday

  • No limit on books!
  • Bargain books are half off, with the option of purchasing a grocery bag for $5 to fill.
  • All rooms open at 11am.

How will this work?

Main room, Saturday only

  • Customers are admitted in the order of their numbered tickets.
  • You may purchase maximum 12 books per visit in first hour.
  • Exit the main room via east door.
  • You may join end of line at north door and reenter if desired.
  • There will be no 12 book limit if there is no line after noon.

    • All other rooms are free to enter at any time.

Anything else?

  • Bring large book donations to main room.
  • Gift certificates are available in $5 dominations.
  • Maximum 160 customers may be in main room at a time.
  • Items for sale are from donations and some items from library that were removed to make room for new purchases.
