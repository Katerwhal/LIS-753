Friends of Palo Alto Library Book Sale

Location

Inside Cubberly Community Center. We will be in three rooms:

  • Main room: Along the north driveway, adjacent to the Charleston shopping center.
  • Children's items: Near the cubberly track, pavilion, and the Greendell school.
  • Bargain books: Rooms K6 and K7.

Hours

Saturday

  • Childrens room and bargain room: opens at 10am.
  • Main room: ticket distribution starts at 8am, room opens at 11am.

Sunday

  • All rooms open at 11am.

Tickets

  • Two numbered tickets will be distributed per person for entry into the main room on Saturday, starting at 8am.
  • You may leave and return before the main room opens and numbers are called, but missing your number being called forfeits your place in line.
  • In this case, or if you don't have a ticket, you may join the latter part of the line.

Prices

  • Book prices are 50 cents or 1 dollar in the main room and children's room.
  • Bargain books are 25 cents, 50 cents, or 1 dollar, with everything in the bargain room being half off on Sunday.
  • Gift certificates are available for purchase in $5 dominations.

Daily Schedule and Specials

Saturday

  • During the first hour of the sale customers may purchase no more than 12 books at a time in the main room and childrens room.
  • The children's room and bargain room are open 1 hour earlier (10am) than the main room (11am).

Saturday, main room only

  • Distribution of numbered tickets starts at 8am.
  • Customers are admitted in the order of their numbered tickets starting at 11am.
  • You may purchase a maximum of 12 books per visit during the first hour.
  • After purchasing, exit the main room through the east door.
  • You may join the end of the line at the north door and reenter if desired.
  • There will be no 12 book limit if there is no line after noon.

Sunday

  • No limit on books!
  • Bargain books are half off, with the option of purchasing a grocery bag for $5 to fill.
  • All rooms open at 11am.

Why buy from us?

  • 100% of proceeds go to benefit the library!
  • No sales tax!
  • Browse over 50,000 items, from music and movies to school-age fiction and non-english books, and much more!
